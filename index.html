Current Research Focus

My work focuses on understanding how wood structure, moisture content, and processing conditions influence the mechanical and durability performance of wood and wood-based products. I am particularly interested in integrating experimental testing with data-driven and machine learning methods to improve prediction of properties such as stiffness, strength, and dimensional stability.

Selected Projects

  • Predicting mechanical properties of structural lumber using acoustic velocity and image-based features.
  • Evaluating the effects of thermal modification on color and mechanical performance of wood.
  • Using non-destructive testing to assess quality and uniformity of forest products.
